# Greatest Common Divisor
> The GCD of two numbers a and b, is the largest positive integer such that it divides both a and b.

## Did you know?
Have you ever noticeed that almost every movie, youtube video, or laptop screen is advertised as **16:9**.

But the actual screen resolution is not 16:9. It's usually 1920*1080.

So how did we get 16:9. We simply divide both numbers by their Greatest Common Divisor.

Everytime you see 16:9, you are actually looking at the result of a GCD computation.

## Approaches of finding the GCD
1: The most Obvious idea - Brute Force <br>

2: Euclidian Algorithm (Subtraction) <br>

3: Euclidian Algorithm (Modulo) <br>
